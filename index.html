Building Together: Ukrainian-led Recovery with Global Support

Our Principles

The URAG supports Ukraine recovery under three principles:

  • Sustainability & Resilience Promoting development that is environmentally responsible, resource-efficient, and socially equitable. Designing cities that will withstand the impacts of nature and man-made disasters.
  • Community Engagement Engaging and involving community members in the planning and design process, fostering collaboration, dialogue, and shared decision-making to ensure that plans reflect the needs and aspirations of local residents.
  • Human Scale & Context-sensitive Designing communities with a focus on human needs and responding to the unique context of each place, respecting local culture, history, and natural features, integrating new development harmoniously into the existing built environment.
